About Us

Kosanovic Engineering Office – independent expertise in structural engineering.

M.Sc. Veljko Kosanovic

Owner · Civil Engineer · aspiring Building Surveyor

With over ten years of hands-on experience in structural building and civil engineering, I provide my clients with independent, practice-oriented consulting – from planning through to final acceptance. My professional career took me from Belgrade to Munich, where I gained extensive experience in turnkey construction as well as the coordination and supervision of all trades involved, first at BAUWENS Construction GmbH & Co. KG and subsequently at Ed. Züblin AG.

My core focus lies in quality assurance, schedule management and technical consulting for private and commercial clients – with a thorough understanding of standards, VOB regulations and current DIN codes.
