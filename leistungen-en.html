Our Services

At the centre is our consulting function. Building on this, we offer the following services.

  • Consulting

    Our core task. Professional and strategic consulting for your project – independent, practical and goal-oriented. From the initial idea through to implementation.

  • Analysis and Assessment

    Expert opinions, arbitration reports, professional assessments – supplemented by consulting recommendations.

  • Planning and Construction Management

    Service specifications, cost estimation, quantity surveys – with consulting support throughout.

  • Quality Assurance

    Plan reviews, construction-phase quality controls – with consulting involvement on request.

  • Documentation and Acceptance

    Evidence preservation, formal acceptances, site inspections – professionally grounded and guided by consulting expertise.

  • Lectures and Seminars

    Technical lectures and seminars on your topics. We are happy to advise you on content and formats.
